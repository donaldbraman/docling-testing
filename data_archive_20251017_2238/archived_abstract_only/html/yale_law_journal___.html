Articles & Essays Webinar: Tips & Tricks for a Successful Submissions Cycle

Jeremy Thomas
25 Jun 2025

In anticipation of our summer submissions cycle reopening on Friday, July 18, the Yale Law Journal is thrilled to host a webinar discussing tips and tricks for a successful submissions cycle. At the webinar, members of our executive board will share what the Journal looks for in a top-notch submission, as well as common pitfalls for scholars to avoid. The webinar will be held on Thursday, July 3 at 7:30 p.m. ET. Please register for the webinar and submit questions in advance here. We will not be able to answer live questions. While the webinar may be particularly helpful for newer scholars, all are welcome to attend. We hope to see you there!
